Our Story

A Family Tradition

The Fudge Plug began in our family kitchen over a decade ago, when Grandma Mary perfected her secret fudge recipe. What started as holiday gifts for friends and neighbors quickly grew into a beloved local business.

Today, we still make every batch by hand using the same traditional methods and high-quality ingredients that made Grandma Mary's fudge famous.
